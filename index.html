Advent Devotional

Let Joy ring out!

Winter’s drab, gray days can be wearying and wearing to many of us. The long weeks leading up to Christmas can become hectic and stressful, but the elements of Advent are simple: a few candles, your Bible, a few close friends and family.

It is our desire that this Advent devotional will resonate in our hearts with Gospel hope and set our hearts to ringing with joy as we celebrate Christ’s first coming and look forward to his second coming.
